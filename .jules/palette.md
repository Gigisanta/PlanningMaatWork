## 2024-04-23 - Expanding Click Targets on Custom Checkboxes
**Learning:** Radix UI / Shadcn `<Label>` components paired with custom `<input type="checkbox">` (or Radix `Checkbox`) frequently lack explicit `htmlFor` <-> `id` linkage by default in rapid prototyping. This results in tiny click targets (just the checkbox box itself) rather than the entire label text, causing poor interaction UX.
**Action:** Always link custom checkboxes and labels with explicit IDs. Additionally, always add `cursor-pointer` to both elements to provide clear visual feedback that the text is an interactive toggle area.
